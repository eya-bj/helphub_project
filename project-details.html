About This Project

Access to clean water is a fundamental human right, yet millions around the world still lack this basic necessity. Your support will help install water filtration systems and dig wells in communities that need them most.

Our Approach

The Clean Water Initiative works with local communities to:

  • Identify areas with the most critical water needs
  • Install sustainable water filtration systems
  • Dig wells in remote communities
  • Educate communities on water conservation and maintenance
  • Monitor and report on water quality improvements

Project Updates

Green Earth Foundation

November 15, 2023

Site Surveys Complete!

We're thrilled to announce that we've completed our site surveys in all three target communities. The local teams have been incredibly supportive and enthusiastic about the project.

The surveys revealed that the water situation is even more critical than initially assessed, with many families walking over 3 miles daily to access water that isn't even clean. This reinforces the importance of our work.

Equipment procurement is underway, and we're on track to begin installations in January. Thank you to all our donors for making this possible!

Posted by John Doe, Project Manager
Green Earth Foundation

October 3, 2023

Project Launch!

We're excited to officially launch our Clean Water Initiative! After months of planning and coordination with local communities, we're ready to begin making a difference.

We've established partnerships with three communities in rural East Africa where access to clean water is severely limited. Our goal is to provide sustainable water solutions that will improve health outcomes and quality of life for over 5,000 people.

Posted by Sarah Johnson, Executive Director
